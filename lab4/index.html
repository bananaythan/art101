Pseudocoding an Everyday Task

  1. Find another car
  2. Connect Jumper Cables
    1. Attach red to red of your battery, then red to red of the other car
    2. Attach black to black of the other car, then black to metal part of dead battery
  3. Start the other Cars Engine
  4. Start the Dead Car
    1. Accelerate a bit on other car if jumpstart does not work after few tries
  5. Take Cables out in Reverse Order
Pseudocoding the Dinosaur Game
  1. Generate a Desert Landscape
    1. Include Cacti in varying groups of 1 to 4
    2. Include Birds
  2. Create Dinosaur Character
    1. Include Animation for Running, Jumping, Ducking, and Death
  3. Implement Controls
    1. Space Button Starts Game and used for Jumping
    2. Down Arrow for Ducking
  4. Create Varibles
    1. Impliment randomizer for Cacti and Bird obstacles
    2. Score begins at 0 and increases by distance Dinosaur goes
